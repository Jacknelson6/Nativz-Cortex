Content Editing Service Agreement · v2026.04.2

1. Services and Deliverables

Anderson Collaborative LLC (4000 Ponce de Leon Blvd, Suite 470, Coral Gables FL 33146) will produce and deliver the content editing services in the tier selected above on a month-to-month basis. Deliverables match the scope described at docs.andersoncollaborative.com/content-editing-packages/, which is incorporated by reference.

2. Payment Terms

Monthly Fee: The Monthly Fee for the selected tier is billed via Stripe on the same day each month. The first month is charged upon signing and is non-refundable once payment is confirmed.

Term: This Agreement renews month-to-month automatically. Either Party may cancel on 30 days written notice to info@andersoncollaborative.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing month; no partial-month refunds.

Tier Changes: Client may change tiers with 15 days written notice. Pro-rata adjustments apply on the next invoice.

Out-of-Scope Work: Billed at $150/hour with prior written approval.

Late Payment: If a Monthly Fee charge fails, Services may be suspended. After two consecutive failed charges, this Agreement terminates automatically.

3. Revisions

Each deliverable within the selected tier includes two rounds of revisions. Additional rounds are billed as out-of-scope under Section 2.

4. Delivery Cadence

Anderson Collaborative delivers the tier's scope on a monthly cadence. Client agrees to provide source material, brand assets, and reviewer feedback within 3 business days of each request. Client-caused delays do not reduce the Monthly Fee or extend the billing month.

5. Ownership and Licenses

Deliverables produced under this Agreement become Client's property upon payment of the Monthly Fee for the month in which they were produced, except for open-source components (retained under their original licenses), generic utilities retained by Anderson Collaborative, and Anderson Collaborative's pre-existing IP.

Anderson Collaborative retains portfolio rights. Client may opt out in writing at any time.

Client represents it owns or has license to all content provided and grants Anderson Collaborative a license to use that content solely to perform the Services.

6. Confidentiality

Each Party will hold the other's non-public information in confidence for 3 years after termination.

7. Data and Privacy

Analytics and customer data collected through Client's properties remain Client's property. Anderson Collaborative acts as a processor (not controller) and will implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures.

8. Warranties and Limitation of Liability

Services performed in a professional and workmanlike manner. Deliverables are "as is" 30 days after final delivery, except for defects reported in writing during that period. Each Party's total liability is capped at the Monthly Fees paid in the prior 12 months. No indirect, consequential, or lost-profit damages.

9. Termination

Either Party may terminate on 30 days written notice. Termination takes effect at the end of the current billing month; the final Monthly Fee is non-refundable. Anderson Collaborative may terminate immediately on two consecutive failed Stripe charges. Sections 5, 6, 7, 8, 10, and 11 survive termination.

10. Electronic Signatures and Audit Trail

Parties consent to electronic execution under the U.S. E-SIGN Act (15 U.S.C. § 7001 et seq.) and Florida UETA (Fla. Stat. § 668.50). Clicking "I Agree and Sign" or drawing your signature below constitutes a legally binding signature.

Anderson Collaborative's system captures as part of the signature record: signer name, email, IP address, user agent, UTC timestamp, selected tier, and SHA-256 hash of the signed PDF. This record is admissible as evidence of execution.

A signed PDF copy is emailed to Client within 1 minute of signing. Anderson Collaborative retains a copy for at least 7 years.

11. General

Governing Law: State of Florida, without regard to conflicts. Venue: Exclusive jurisdiction in Miami-Dade County, FL courts. Entire Agreement: This replaces all prior agreements on the same subject. Amendments: Must be in writing and signed by both Parties. Notices: Via email with delivery confirmation to info@andersoncollaborative.com and Client's email above.
